Our Work

Our mission is to bring fresh food and stop food insecurity throughout Brooklyn.


Here are some of our past initiatives:

  • Collected over 3,000 pounds of food from farms, restaurants, grocers, and manufacturers to help feed struggling New Yorkers.
  • Opened 20 emergency food distribution sites during the COVID-19 pandemic to provide fresh groceries and meals to those in need.
  • Rescued over 20 thousand pounds of food that would have otherwise gone to waste to help provide fresh food to New Yorkers.


Our work would not be possible without the generous contributions of our supporters! We thank you for your support!
